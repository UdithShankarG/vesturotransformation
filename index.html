How It Works

The AI-engineered pipeline behind every prompt

01

Subject Analysis

AI deeply analyzes your subject — its anatomy, mechanical structure, proportions, and which parts can be decomposed into foldable metallic components.

02

Abstract Engineering

Parts are reverse-engineered into a compact geometric abstract — tightly folded, interlocking metallic chips that hide the final form completely.

03

Deployment Choreography

Each part gets a unique unfolding sequence — pivots, rotations, snap-fits — with variable speeds for accurate vs. corrective movements.

04

Prompt Synthesis

A frame-by-frame, timestamped video prompt is generated with strict rules, negative constraints, and cinematic raw-footage specifications.
